【趣味研究】多跳问答的难点与挑战

问题汇总

1 数据集

  1. 知识更新滞后：
  • 问答数据集的答案过时：目前常用的问答数据集采集和创建的时间早于 LLM 预训练和发布的时间；
  • 向量数据库的知识过时：向量数据库的知识采集和创建也是来源于较早的百科知识库。
  1. 问题实体歧义：常用问答数据集的问题十分简短，实体能指代的对象会有多个，比如人物重名、作品形式多样、同义词等等，会使得 LLM 的回答与检索文档的方向产生偏差，无法针对性作答。如问题 What genre is Mother? 其实是想问音乐的类型，但是 LLM 回答的是电影的类型。

2 检索

  1. 干扰信息多：庞大的向量数据库会导致检索到很多相似的文档块，但无法保证与问题的相关性，因此反而会干扰 LLM 的判断。
  2. 检索方式不匹配：无论是稀疏检索、密集检索还是混合检索，不同的数据集适合的方式并不一致，有些数据集（2WikiMultiHopQA、PopQA、TriviaQA）更适合关键词，有些数据集（ASQA、NaturalQuestions）更适合语义。常用问答数据集的问题都非常简短，语义并不丰富，难以针对性检索，比如 PopQA 数据集：

3 LLM

  1. 胡编乱造：LLM 在缺乏信息时会自信地生成错误答案，因此需要精心设计提示词。比如 LLM 基本上答不对人物职业和出生地的相关问题，而且这类问题也很难检索到正确答案。
  2. 解码策略不稳定：
  • 未确定采样策略下，提问 LLM 会得到各种随机的答案，而有时会出现正确答案。
  • 确定采样策略下（比如贪心采样），随着生成长度的增加，未经过指令微调的 LLM 的回答质量严重下降，甚至泄露预训练信息。

4 评估方式

  1. 答案多义：LLM 答案可能与标准答案意思一致但表达不同，却被判错。
  2. 匹配方式僵化：评估时仅做字符串匹配 any(answer in normalize_answer(response) for answer in answers if answer)，忽略语义等价性。

案例分析

例1

Question

Who has the highest goals in world football?

Gold Answer

The players with the highest all-time goals and highest men’s and women’s international football goals differ. The player with the highest all-time men’s football goals is Josef Bican, who in 2020 was recognized by FIFA, the international governing body of football, as the record scorer with an estimated 805 goals. Christine Sinclair has the highest goals in women’s international football with 187 and is the all-time leader for international goals scored for men or women. Cristiano Ronaldo and Ali Daei are currently tied for leading goalscorer in the history of men’s international football with 109.

评估时标准化后的答案列表：[‘daei’, ‘ali daei’, ‘bican’, ‘josef bican’, ‘sinclair’, ‘christine sinclair’]，并没有包含 Cristiano Ronaldo。

Response

The highest goalscorer in world football is Josef Bican, an Austrian-Czech footballer who scored 805 goals in 530 games between 1931 and 1955. However, this record is not officially recognized by FIFA, as it is based on incomplete data and may include friendly matches and unofficial games.
The top 10 highest goalscorers in world football, according to FIFA, are:

  1. Cristiano Ronaldo (Portugal) - 755 goals in 974 games
  2. Josef Bican (Austria/Czech Republic) - 805 goals in 530 games (not officially recognized by FIFA)
  3. Ferenc Puskás (Hungary) - 746 goals in 629 games
  4. Gerd Müller (Germany) - 735 goals in 706 games
  5. Pelé (Brazil) - 725 goals in 656 games
  6. Romário (Brazil) - 714 goals in 744 games

Now Answer

Cristiano Ronaldo (938)、Lionel Messi (865)、Josef Bican (805)。

  1. 电视节目：Football’s Greatest；
  2. 游戏：FIFA (video game series)；
  3. 西班牙足球对抗赛：Spanish football rivalries；
  4. C 罗的庆祝动作：Cristiano Ronaldo；

例2

Question

How many times have the lakers won the finals?

Gold Answer

这个问题来源于 ASQA 数据集，它有两个答案列表（其他数据集不存在），分别是 QA 的答案和 QA Pairs 的答案列表。

The Los Angeles Lakers are an American professional basketball team based in Los Angeles. The Lakers compete in the National Basketball Association, NBA, as a member of the league’s Western Conference Pacific Division. The Lakers are one of the most successful teams in the history of the NBA, since 2010 the Lakers won the championship finals 16 times, they won their 17th in 2020.

Response

由于问题本身并没有限定时间，LLM 的回答是 17 次，属于回答错误。

例3

Question

这个作品有电影、百老汇的两种艺术形式，因此并不清楚问题询问的是扮演者还是角色介绍。

Gold Answer

There are several versions of Fiddler on the Roof. The original Broadway production of the show, which opened in 1964, had the first musical theatre run in history to surpass 3,000 performances. The character of Fruma Sarah, a ghostly depiction of the late wife of Lazar Wolf, is played by Carol Sawyer Yussel. In the 1971 film, Fiddler on the Roof, which theatrically released on November 3, 1971, by United Artists to critical and commercial success, Ruth Madoc played Fruma Sarah. In the 2015-2016 Broadway Revival of Fiddler on the Roof, American singer and actress Jessica Vosk played Fruma Sarah.

Response

LLM 专注于对这个作品的介绍，也提到了答案，不过匹配失败。
